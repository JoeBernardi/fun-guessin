Senior JavaScript Developer Tech Interview 2

STEP 1: Guess a number between 1 and 2000.

This URL will accept a POST param called `number`. Its response will be a `status` of "low", "high", "correct", or "out of bounds". If there's a problem with your request, the `status` will be "invalid post."
Based on those responses, your task is figuring out what number the server is thinking of. You're more than welcome to use Stack Overflow/the internet and the AJAX library of your choice.
